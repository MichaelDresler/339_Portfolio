In this second-year information design class, we worked on a 5-week project to create a captivating microsite for Dutch Design Week, an annual design conference. Our main objective was to develop an expressive microsite that effectively showcased the event. The first three weeks involved extensive visual experimentation, exploring different design qualities and aesthetics.
