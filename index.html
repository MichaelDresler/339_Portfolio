About

Hi, I’m Michael, an interaction and motion designer who loves creating unique projects. Currently a student at Simon Fraser University’s Interactive Arts and Technology program, I am committed to improving my design skills by delving into its history, dissecting the methodologies of past designers, and understanding the contextual evolution that shapes today's design landscape. I recognize that growth emerges from the exploration of the discipline's foundations, acknowledging the challenges and successes of those who have paved the way.
